About Us

SudaVerse is building a digital future where Sudanese dialects are integrated into AI systems. We collect, process, and preserve authentic linguistic data, ensuring Sudan’s voices are represented globally.

Our Vision

We envision inclusive AI that protects privacy, preserves identity, and empowers interaction in Sudanese dialects. Our open-source model is a reference for researchers and a catalyst for innovation.

Objectives

• Build an open-source LLM for Sudanese dialects
• Archive authentic text and audio data
• Enable AI applications in local dialects
• Strengthen cultural identity and justice
• Support research, startups, and innovation
• Provide a regional model for preservation

Why SudaVerse Matters

Language is identity, memory, and culture. Without digital representation, dialects risk fading away. SudaVerse ensures Sudanese dialects thrive in the AI age.

  • Identity

    Preserves Sudanese cultural identity for generations.

  • Empowerment

    Ensures Sudanese voices shape global AI systems.

  • Innovation

    Provides tools for AI applications in local dialects.

  • Heritage

    Creates a comprehensive archive of texts and audio.

  • Collaboration

    Open-source model inviting global research and input.

  • Regional Impact

    Sets a standard for dialect preservation across Africa.
