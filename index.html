About Us

SudaVerse is building a digital future where Sudanese dialects are integrated into AI systems. We collect, process, and preserve authentic linguistic data, ensuring Sudan’s voices are represented globally.

Our Vision

We envision inclusive AI that protects privacy, preserves identity, and empowers interaction in Sudanese dialects. Our open-source model is a reference for researchers and a catalyst for innovation.

Objectives

We focus on six core goals to preserve and empower Sudanese dialects:

Open LLM

Build and release an open-source language model for Sudanese dialects.

Heritage Archive

Collect and preserve authentic text and audio to safeguard Sudan’s memory.

AI Applications

Enable tools that understand and produce Sudanese dialects for daily use.

Cultural Identity

Ensure fair representation of Sudanese voices in global AI systems.

Research & Startups

Provide resources for innovation, helping Sudanese entrepreneurs compete globally.

Regional Model

Set a replicable standard for dialect preservation across Africa and the Arab world.
